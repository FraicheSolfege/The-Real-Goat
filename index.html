The Legend of Link

(the real hero)

The Legend of Zelda series is a popular action-adventure video game franchise created by Nintendo. It was first introduced in 1986 and has since become one of the most iconic and influential series in the gaming industry. The protagonist of the series is Link, a courageous and skilled hero who embarks on a quest to rescue Princess Zelda and thwart the plans of the main antagonist, Ganon.

Shigeru Miyamoto, the creator of such beloved series as The Legend of Zelda, Mario, Donkey Kong, and Star Fox
Link, the titular hero and an elf of few words. He is known for his iconic green tunic and pointed hat, which have become symbols of the franchise. He is typically equipped with a sword and shield, and throughout the games, he acquires various magical items and tools to aid him on his quest. His adventures often involve solving puzzles, battling enemies, and exploring dungeons to gather powerful artifacts.
